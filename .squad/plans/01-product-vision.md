# Eventy Product Vision

Eventy is a wedding invitation platform.

Users can:

- Browse invitation templates
- Select a template
- Create a wedding invitation
- Customize invitation details
- Publish invitations
- Share invitations through a public URL
- Collect RSVP responses
- Collect guest wishes

Primary Users:

- Bride
- Groom
- Wedding Planner

Secondary Users:

- Wedding Guests
